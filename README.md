# ANSKAR — Sheet Metal Products Manufacturer in Riga, Latvia

**Website:** [anskar.lv](https://anskar.lv)

ANSKAR is a professional sheet metal manufacturer based in Riga, Latvia. The company specialises in producing high-quality custom sheet metal products for residential and commercial buildings — including parapets, window sills, gutters, chimney caps, snow barriers, wind edges, roof ridges, and more. Every product is made to individual dimensions and customer specifications, ensuring a precise fit and long-lasting performance.

## About the Website

The ANSKAR website is available in three languages — **Latvian (LV)**, **Russian (RU)**, and **English (EN)** — making it accessible to a wide range of customers across the Baltic region and beyond.

### Products — [anskar.lv/products.html](https://anskar.lv/products.html)

The products page presents the full catalogue of ANSKAR's sheet metal offerings. Products are organised into clearly defined categories:

- **Window Sills** — Standard and custom-sized galvanised or painted window sills
- **Gutters** — Half-round and rectangular rainwater gutters
- **Parapets** — Galvanised and powder-coated parapets for flat roofs
- **Post & Chimney Caps** — Standard, square, and custom chimney caps for weather protection
- **Snow Barriers** — Lattice-type snow barriers for roof safety
- **Roof Ridges** — Standard roof ridges and folded ridge flashing
- **Ventilation Grilles** — Rectangular ventilation grilles for facade ventilation
- **Chimney Installation Nodes** — Standard and pitched-roof chimney sleeves
- **Chimney Casings** — Painted chimney casing assemblies
- **Wind Edges** — Standard and profiled wind edge trims
- **Pipe Bends** — 45° and 90° pipe bends for drainage systems
- **Ventilation Outlets** — Roof ventilation pipes
- **Roof Hatches** — Standard roof access hatches
- **Chimney Finishing** — Complete chimney finishing kits
- **Custom Sheet Metal Products** — Bespoke fabrication to individual drawings and dimensions

Each product can be filtered by category. Customers can upload their own technical drawings or sketches directly on the product page and add items to an inquiry cart.
